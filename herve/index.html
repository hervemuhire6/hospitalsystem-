Serivisi nziza ku buzima bwawe

About This System

This system helps people book appointments easily without going to the hospital first. It saves time and makes the process simple for both patients and staff.

How It Works

  1. Register on the system (Kwiyandikisha)
  2. Choose a doctor (Guhitamo muganga)
  3. Book an appointment (Gusaba randevu)
  4. Come to hospital on your date
  5. Get help and go home

Services We Offer

  • General check
  • Simple treatment
  • Health advice
  • Emergency help
